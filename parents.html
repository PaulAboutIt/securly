Features



1:1 Education Analytics

Securly provides cloud based Big Data analytics designed to improve your child’s productivity and achievement. Parents can now analyze their kids’ activity across educational sites and time sinks, in-school and at-home across school provided 1:1 devices such as iPads, Nexus tablets and Chromebooks.



Coming Soon - 1:1 Device Management

Parents can now become the IT admins of the 1:1 devices at home, opening up sites for supervised access that are otherwise blocked by the district. E.g. if the district blocks online games, Twitter, and YouTube – parents can open these up for supervised access at home.
